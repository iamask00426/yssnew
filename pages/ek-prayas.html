Ek Prayas. One Effort, Infinite Impact.

Ek Prayas is a focused employment support initiative under YSS. It is built on the belief that helping someone access work, stability, and opportunity can create real change not only for one person, but for an entire family and community.

E
Employment Support Helping people move toward dignified work and stronger livelihood direction.

Livelihood Access Focused on opportunity, connection, and practical support for job seekers.
  • Connect job aspirants with meaningful opportunities.
  • Support equal access, confidence, and long-term progress.
  • Strengthen families and communities through livelihood impact.

What It Stands For

Ek Prayas focuses on helping people move toward employment and livelihood support. It is about connecting individuals with work opportunities, practical guidance, and a stronger path toward self-reliance.

Why It Matters

Employment creates dignity, stability, and long-term confidence. By supporting access to work and opportunity, Ek Prayas turns social service into lasting impact that can improve lives beyond a single moment.
